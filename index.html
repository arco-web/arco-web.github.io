ArcoLinux: a learning project

Invest your time in ArcoLinux and we will invest in you. Improve your Linux skills by following the 1-2 year development plan. Which is why we have the motto:

“Learn, Have Fun, and Enjoy.”

Configurability
Easy and widely configurable setup process that comes with applications, bluetooth, printer support, and more.
Performance
Low resource consumption and blazing fast performance regardless of system specs.
Community
Help is always available from the ArcoLinux, Arch Linux, and general Linux community.
Rolling Release
Keep up with (optionally) frequent updates.
Multilanguage
Available in many languages including Spanish, Galician, Catalan, English, German, and more.
